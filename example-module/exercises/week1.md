# Week 1 Exercises

## Question 1
Let \( V = \mathbb{R}^3 \) and \( W = \{(x, y, z) \in \mathbb{R}^3 : x + y + z = 0\} \).

(a) Show that \( W \) is a subspace of \( V \).
(b) Find a basis for \( W \).
(c) What is \( \dim(W) \)?

## Question 2
Let \( T: \mathbb{R}^2 \to \mathbb{R}^2 \) be defined by \( T(x, y) = (2x + y, x - y) \).

(a) Show that \( T \) is a linear map.
(b) Find the matrix of \( T \) with respect to the standard basis.
(c) Find \( \ker(T) \) and \( \text{im}(T) \).

## Question 3
Determine whether the following sets are linearly independent in \( \mathbb{R}^3 \):

(a) \( \{(1, 0, 1), (0, 1, 1), (1, 1, 0)\} \)
(b) \( \{(1, 2, 3), (4, 5, 6), (7, 8, 9)\} \)
